Fama–French Alpha Signal Generation

  • Backtested the strategy from 2014–2025 by dynamically allocating portfolio weights to U.S. equities from alpha signals, achieving an 8.5% annual growth rate; applies MCMC for evolving alpha estimates across market regimes.
